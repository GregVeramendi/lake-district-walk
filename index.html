Logistics & notes

Trains

  • Arrive Windermere 19:40, Fri 3 July.
  • Return: 08:39 from Corkickle to Lancaster, Fri 10 July.
  • Corkickle station is a 10–15 min walk from Whitehaven harbour.

Accommodation

  • Grasmere YHA & Borrowdale YHA — to book.
  • Black Sail YHA (x2 nights) — booked.
  • Ennerdale YHA — booked.
  • Whitehaven (Thu 9 July) — B&B / hotel to book.
  • All YHA Lake District hostels

Good to pack

  • Waterproofs & warm layers — mountain weather changes fast.
  • Proper walking boots with ankle support for the passes.
  • OS map & compass / offline GPS — Black Sail and Ennerdale have patchy signal.
  • Cash + snacks: Black Sail is remote with no shops nearby.
  • Refillable water bottle; plenty of becks to top up from.

Optional summits from Black Sail

  • Haystacks (597 m) — Wainwright’s favourite fell.
  • Great Gable (899 m) and Pillar (892 m).
  • Red Pike (826 m) & Steeple (819 m) ridge.
